What is Project Management

Project management is defined as the process of steering a project from the start through its lifecycle. The main objective of project management is to complete a project within the established goals of time, budget, and quality. Project management methods can be applied to any project. It is often tailored to a specific type of project based on project size, nature, industry or sector.
